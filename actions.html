What Can You Do?

You don't have to go all out to help combat aginst climate change. Here are a five easy things an average human can do to fight against climate change.

1. Use less energy. As of 2019, 29 out of the 50 US states has less than 15% of their energy come from renewables. By unplugging devices, turning off lights, and making sure not to waste energy, you can limit the use of non-renewable, greenhouse gas creating energy. (Dunbar) (Stebbins)

2. Consider biking to places nearby. This also reduces greehouse gasses.

3. Change lightbulbs to LED. These bulbs use up to 75% less energy than normal light bulbs. (Sission)

4. Wash your clothes in cold water. (Sission)

5. Recycle! (Sission)
