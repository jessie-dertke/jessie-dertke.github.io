What Can You Do?

You don't have to go all out to help combat aginst climate change. Here are a few things an average human can do to fight against climate change.

1. Use less energy. As of 2019, 29 out of the 50 US states has less than 15% of their energy come from renewables. By unplugging devices, turning off lights, and making sure not to waste energy, you can limit the use of non-renewable, CO2 creating energy.

2. Consider biking to places nearby. Cars emitt CO2, so by limiting your usage, you are helping the enviroment.
